# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 7, 2026**

## Overview
Hermes Agent and its associated tools (API Relay, Memory Installer, Vibe Coding) are developer tools that run locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
- **We do not collect** any personally identifiable information
- **We do not use** cookies, trackers, or analytics
- **We do not transmit** your data to any third party
- **We do not store** any user data on our servers

## Local-Only Operation
All API keys, credentials, and configuration files are stored exclusively on your local machine. No data leaves your environment except when you explicitly use the tools to interact with third-party services (e.g., posting content to social media platforms via your own API keys).

## Third-Party Services
When you use Hermes Agent to interact with third-party services (LinkedIn, GitHub, Medium, Telegram, etc.), those interactions are governed by the respective platform's privacy policies. Hermes Agent acts solely as a local interface to those services.
